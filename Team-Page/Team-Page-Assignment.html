Meet the team!

Unc

Uncle himothy, AKA Unc, is both a teacher and student. The ways of the Sazón are strong in him, but he recognizes the benefits of accepting counsel and always looking for ways to improve. He always takes this into consideration when training his students such as Monkey and Double T.

Monkey

A relative of Unc, Monkey has a high Sazónaclorian count. His training is helping him to use his Sazón pa' meterle presión. Currently, he is putting his training into practice out in the field and is bringing back good results. He is the newest member of the Presionistas

Double T

Double T was one of the founding members of the Presionistas along with Unc, 2 Sheisty, and Too Nasty. He is not as powerful as the other Presión masters, but he is a contributer to the group nonetheless. He is currently receiving training from every memeber of the Presionistas in order to more fully develop his Sazón.

Too Nasty

Partner of 2 Sheisty, Too Nasty is the most experienced of the group and has a Sazónachlorian count so high that it broke the Presionometer. She along with her counterpart 2 Sheisty have been metiéndole presión their whole lives. Too Nasty is like a cup of Bustelos, strong, sweet, and flavorful. Good by itself, but when paired up with that leche hits a whole nother nivel.

2 Sheisty

The counterpart of Too Nasty, 2 Sheisty fulfills the role of Leche with that Café. She not only compliments, but adds to the overall flavor, enhancing the Sazón levels of whoever she is around. As she is the partner of Too Nasty, she has a lot of experience as well.

The PResionistas, established in October of 2024 were a group of people joined by the ties of Sazón in order to share the gift of Presión with the world. Constantly seeking to improve themselves, they stay a tight knit group of individuals who stop, collaborate, and listen in order to enhance each other's skills. Since their founding, their levels of Presión have increased exponentially, and they have added new members to their cause. Their message to the world: Stay rizzy
