Where are the tacos?!

Brand new in Carlsbad California, off of El Camino Real and Alga, and right across from Vons and Black Rail, Los Tacos Mexican food and Beer Garden has just opened! This is one of their many locations, but definetly the newest! The official address is:
6981 El Camino Real
UNIT 101,
Carlsbad, CA 92009

Menu

Obviously, tacos. But they offer a variety of meats including Carne Asada, Birria, Pollo, and Adobada. Additionally, they have multiple refreshment options like their aguas frescas and fountain drinks. Also, this location has a beer garden, meaning that they serve alchohol to those who are over 21 years of age.

Catering

Los Tacos also has catering options. Check their official website for further details. They have catered to different events, offering large portions of food, allowing everybody to eat to satisfaction and choose what to make.

Discounts

Sometimes they will have offers, or special reduced price days, but if you are a student at Mira Costa Community College, you recieve a 10% discount when you show your ID in person.

Atmosphere and Ratings

Los Tacos is a mexican restaurant that displays that culture in their atmoshpere. It is a casual restaurant, with a polite and clean atmosphere. The workers also enjoy a laid back schedule.

Naw G I work Wednesday,Thursday,Friday 830am-4:30pm

Their rating of 4.6 stars shows how much the community loves this restaurant.
